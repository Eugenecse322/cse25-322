Who We Are

The Desert Resort

The Desert Resort is an organisation dedicated to bringing the very best of what African tourism has to offer. We welcome diverse guests from all over the world for a once-in-a-lifetime experience they will never forget.

Our primary objective is to provide a seamless digital and physical gateway for luxury desert tourism — for both international visitors and local travellers seeking an extraordinary escape.

Founded on the principles of exclusivity, conservation, and authentic African hospitality, we have become a benchmark for luxury desert experiences across the continent.
