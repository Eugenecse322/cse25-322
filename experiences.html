What Awaits You

Desert
Experiences

From adrenaline-fuelled adventures to serene nights under a canopy of stars — curated for the discerning traveller.

🐪
Camel Safari
Journey across golden dunes on a guided camel safari, following ancient desert trade routes as the sun paints the sky at dusk. Our expert guides share stories of the desert that have been passed down through generations.
Duration: 2–4 hours
⭐
Stargazing Nights
With zero light pollution, the desert offers one of Africa's most spectacular night skies. Our resident astronomers guide you through the cosmos with state-of-the-art telescopes and an intimate dinner under the stars.
Duration: 2–3 hours
🏎️
Dune Bashing Tours
Strap in for an exhilarating 4x4 dune-bashing adventure across towering sand mountains guided by our expert desert drivers. An adrenaline-charged experience that will leave you breathless.
Duration: 3 hours
Desert Spa
Rejuvenate with traditional African treatments inspired by the desert landscape. Our therapists use locally sourced minerals and botanicals to restore, revitalise, and reconnect you with the earth.
Duration: 60–120 min
🌅
Sunrise Hike
Wake before dawn and ascend the highest dune as the first light of day floods the horizon in breathtaking shades of amber and rose. A meditative experience that stays with you long after you leave.
Duration: 2 hours
🍷
Desert Sundowner
Toast the day's end with sundowner cocktails atop a dune as the sky transforms into a canvas of colour. Includes canapes, live acoustic music, and stories from your host guide.
